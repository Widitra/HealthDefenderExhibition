How To Play Health Defender
Game Objective

Defend your body against waves of invading pathogens by strategically placing defense towers along the path.

Tower Types
Y
Antibody Tower (25 Energy)

Basic defense tower with moderate range and damage. Effective against most pathogens.

C
Vitamin Tower (40 Energy)

Boosts your immune system. Slower attack but has a longer range and deals more damage.

T
Immune Cell (60 Energy)

Advanced defense with area damage. Can hit multiple pathogens at once.

How to Play
  1. Click the "Start Game" button to begin the battle against pathogens
  2. Select a defense tower from the Command Center panel
  3. Click on a green placement spot to build your selected tower
  4. Your towers will automatically target and attack nearby pathogens
  5. Each defeated pathogen gives you energy to build more towers
  6. Don't let the pathogens reach the end of the path or you'll lose health
  7. Strategically place different types of towers to counter various pathogens
  8. Survive all waves to win and protect your body!
